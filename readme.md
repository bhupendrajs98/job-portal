# Hireon - Job Portal

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**Hireon** is a full-stack MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) job portal platform designed to connect job seekers with employers seamlessly. It is inspired by real-world platforms like Indeed and LinkedIn, focusing on user-friendly job searching, posting, and application management.

### For Job Seekers:
- **Browse Jobs**: Search jobs by title, company, or location.
- **Apply to Jobs**: Submit applications with resume uploads.
- **Profile Management**: Create and update professional profiles.
- **Saved Jobs**: Save favorite job listings for future reference.

### For Employers:
- **Post Jobs**: Add new job listings with details like description, requirements, and salary.
- **Manage Applications**: View applicants and manage hiring pipeline.
- **Company Profile**: Showcase company details and branding.

### General Features:
- **Authentication & Authorization**: JWT-based login for secure access.
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop and mobile.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Manage users, jobs, and site content.
